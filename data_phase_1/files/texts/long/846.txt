It’s a worrying time and brings out much anxiety. You realise how much you take for granted being able to see family and go out whenever you want. It’s also worrying in terms of work and economy - will small family run businesses survive? How long will it take them to build back up and can they with so many people losing jobs, losing income etc?
How long will we have to stay in lockdown? What will the impact be on many things. But it also brings out what people really feel and who are really important to them. You really do find out who your friends are and how very important your family are - staying in touch is so important.
