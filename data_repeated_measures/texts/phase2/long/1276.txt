The corona virus pandemic has prevented us from doing many things, and has take people away from us. Staying indoors as the government instructed to has caused the mental health of myself and many others to degrade over time. Working and studying at home has made studying at home quite difficult.
On the flip side, it has given all of us much more free time which many of us spent of hobbies and learning new things of which some may help us advance in our careers in the future. We have become more aware of the importance of mental health and keeping contact with friends and family.
