It feels like we've all been driving along on a coach journey and suddenly we all have to get off and sit in the service station, for an unknown period of time. No-one can tell us when we can get back on the coach again, and continue as before. 
The days start to roll into one another and you lose your footing, but at the same time you're enjoying the quiet, and the slower pace of life, while missing the things you usually do. 
Italy is worrying, with its disproportionately high number of victims. And also the PM being ill and hospitalised.
